About Us

Recruitment Innovation for Student Empowerment (RISE) is a non-profit consulting group committed to providing students preparing for college education with professional guidance; to make informed decisions about their career path.

RISE partners with academia, families, communities, corporations, and institutions in seeking financial support and leveraging available resources towards a RISE scholar's college education.

RISE collaborates with students, faculty, and staff to identify obstacles, provide early intervention and/or services to empower students in attaining their educational goals.

RISE provides structured programs, leadership training, and continued mentoring that guarantees college graduation for RISE scholars.

Foundation

Mr. Forster is a graduate of the University of Oregon. As a former Director of Financial Aid Director, College Retention, and Upward Bound, Mr. Forster worked with institutions of higher learning. He has the contacts, working knowledge and expertise to assist any student who is serious in their pursuit of a 2 or 4 year degree.

Mr. Forster served in various capacities at an HBCU for nine years. He personally oversees the program and keeps abreast of the progress of each of his students. Parents are invited and welcome to be part of the process.
