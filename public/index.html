Recruitment Innovation for Student Empowerment (RISE) is a non-profit consulting group committed to providing students preparing for college education with professional guidance; to make informed decisions about their career path.

RISE partners with academia, families, communities, corporations, and institutions in seeking financial support and leveraging available resources towards a RISE scholar's college education. RISE collaborates with students, faculty, and staff to identify obstacles, provide early intervention and/or services to empower students in attaining their educational goals.
